Portlet One

There are 6 tools that you can add:

REMOVE: a confirmation box will appear to confirm deleting.

SLIDE UP: portlets will slide up / down. Just header will stay.

MAXIMIZE: portlets will get all the page width and height.

POP UP: portlets will "detach" from page, that permit move it where you want.

SETTINGS: a dropdown menu that permit you adding other options.

RELOAD: permit to reload portlets content for statistics, messages...
